Methodology

Experimental Design

  • Baseline: 10 runs × 1000 ticks, random station placement
  • AI Fleet: 10 runs × 1000 ticks, GA-optimised stations
  • Seed parity: Identical event seeds for fair comparison
  • Metric: Average Response Time (arrival_tick − spawn_tick)
  • Statistical test: Paired two-tailed Student's t-test

Limitations

Model Simplifications

  • Single map topology (Model Town) — not tested on other cities
  • Uniform Poisson event rate — real demand has spatial/temporal patterns
  • Ambulances move at constant speed — no acceleration or traffic signals
  • Events resolved in fixed ticks — no variable treatment time
  • No hospital transport modelled — ambulance returns to base after scene

Technical Constraints

  • GA uses single-objective fitness — no multi-criteria optimisation
  • HDBSCAN is custom-built — may differ from scikit-learn reference
  • Distance matrix is pre-computed — doesn't reflect live congestion
  • Sensitivity sweep tested 215 configs — not exhaustive tuning
  • Pygame rendering under SDL dummy may not reflect true visual FPS

Future Work

  • Multi-city graph support & real-world OSM data
  • Reinforcement learning for dynamic dispatch decisions
  • Hospital routing, capacity modelling & patient handoff
